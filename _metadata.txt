Week 3 Dataset: Orders 2023 (Quarterly)
Rows per quarter: 50000
Total rows: 200000
Columns: order_id, customer_id, product_id, category, subcategory, region, status,
         order_year, order_month, order_day, price, quantity, discount, extended_price,
         blob_a, blob_b, blob_c, optional_score
Design notes: Wide table with large text blobs and low-cardinality fields to accentuate
             Parquet projection and predicate pushdown benefits over CSV.
Skew: Customer distribution uses Pareto to emulate heavy hitters.
Temporal: Orders spread across each quarter of 2023.
Usage: Students convert CSV to Parquet and compare eager vs lazy execution in Polars.
